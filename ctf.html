Event Overview:

The Security BSides Dehradun 0x01 CTF, themed "Death Note (Shinigami Riddle CTF)", will be an exciting offline event held on September 7th, 2024. The CTF will challenge participants with a series of cybersecurity puzzles inspired by the popular anime series, Death Note.

The competition will feature 20 challenges of varying difficulty, ranging from beginner to expert levels. Participants will need to use their skills in web security, cryptography, reverse engineering, binary exploitation, forensics, and more to solve the challenges and earn points. The CTF will be a great opportunity for cybersecurity professionals, enthusiasts, and students to test their skills and learn new techniques in a fun and competitive environment. The event will be held offline at Graphic Era Hill University.

Register now to secure your spot and embark on an unforgettable journey into the world of cybersecurity challenges!

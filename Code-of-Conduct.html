Code of Conduct

BSides Dehradun is a security conference dedicated to fostering open communication, collaboration, and learning within the information security community. We are committed to creating a safe and inclusive environment for all participants, regardless of background, experience, or affiliation. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for behavior and outlines the consequences of violating these expectations.

Values

  • Openness and Transparency: We encourage open communication and discussion of security topics, even if they are controversial. We value transparency and expect participants to be honest and upfront about their intentions and activities.



  • Respect and inclusion: We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment for all participants. We expect everyone to treat each other with respect, regardless of background, experience, or affiliation. We have zero tolerance for discrimination or harassment of any kind.



  • collaboration and learning: We believe that the best way to learn and grow is through collaboration. We encourage participants to share their knowledge and expertise with others.



  • Safety and security: We are committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all participants. We expect everyone to follow the conference rules and guidelines, and to report any suspicious activity to the conference organizers.

Prohibited Behavior

The following behavior is strictly prohibited at Bsides Dehradun:

  • violence or threats of violence: We encourage open communication and discussion of security topics, even if they are controversial. We value transparency and expect participants to be honest and upfront about their intentions and activities.



  • harassment: This includes any unwelcome conduct that is based on a person's race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability, or any other protected class. Examples of harassment include, but are not limited to, offensive comments, unwanted touching, stalking, and photography or recording without consent.



  • discrimination: We have zero tolerance for  discrimination of any kind. This includes, but is not limited to, discrimination based on race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability, or any other protected class.



  • doxing or other privacy violations: Sharing  private informationabout another person without their consent is strictly prohibited.



  • Spam or unsolicited commercial activity:Sending unsolicited commercial emails or engaging in other forms of spam is prohibited.



  • Destruction of property: Any behavior that disrupts the conference or interferes with the ability of others to participate is prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to, loud talking, arguing, and using offensive language.



  • Unprofessional conduct: Any behavior that is unprofessional or disrespectful to the conference organizers, volunteers, or other participants is prohibited.



  • Use of illegal drugs or alcohol: The use of  illegal drugs or excessive alcohol consumptionis prohibited.

Consequences of Violation

Any participant who violates this Code of Conduct may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including expulsion from the conference without refund. In addition, the conference organizers may report any illegal activity to the appropriate authorities.

Reporting Violation

If you experience or witness any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the conference organizers immediately.  You can report violations in person to a conference organizer or volunteer, or by sending an email to support@bsidesdehradun.com

Additional Notes

  • This Code of Conduct is subject to change at any time. Please review the Code of Conduct regularly for updates.



  • The conference organizers reserve the right to refuse admission to or remove from the conference any person who they believe is likely to violate this Code of Conduct.



  • This Code of Conduct is in addition to, and does not supersede, any other applicable laws or regulations.



Photography & Videography Guidelines

As Bsides Dehradun is a public event,  no restrictions are placed on photography or videography. Attendees (including organizers, volunteers, sponsors, and media) are welcome to capture photos and videos during the conference. However, with open access comes the responsibility to  respect the privacy and comfort of others.

Remember, this is a community event, and we encourage everyone to use common sense and good judgment. If someone doesn't want their picture taken, please respect their wishes.

As with most conferences, Bsides Dehradun reserves the right to use images and videos captured during the event in future marketing materials. This includes photos with your likeness.

For attendees who prefer not to be photographed:

  • Discreetly pick up a "No photos, please"sticker at the registration desk.



  • Place the  sticker prominently on your badge or clothing where it's easily visible.



Your comfort and privacy are our priority. By following these guidelines, we can ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience at Bsides Dehradun.

We encourage all participants to help us create a safe and inclusive environment for everyone at Bsides Dehradun. Thank you for your cooperation.

Experience the Beauty and Culture of Dehradun

A Place Where Tech & Nature Meets!

Dear BSides Dehradun attendees,

We're delighted to welcome you to the beautiful city of Dehradun! Nestled in the Doon Valley amidst the foothills of the Himalayas, Dehradun is a place of scenic beauty and rich heritage.For those visiting for the first time, get ready for an unforgettable experience as you balance cutting-edge cybersecurity discussions with the chance to explore the charms of this Himalayan gateway. And bonus - we're conveniently located within easy reach of beloved destinations like Mussoorie and Rishikesh.

Getting There

By Air

Dehradun's Jolly Grant Airport (DED) has frequent connections to major Indian cities.

By Train

Dehradun Railway Station offers excellent connectivity to various parts of India.

By Road

Well-maintained highways connect Dehradun with Delhi and other nearby cities, making road trips scenic and convenient.

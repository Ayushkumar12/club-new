Graphic Era Hill University, Dehradun

566/6, Bell Road, Society Area, Clement Town,
Dehradun, Bharu Wala Grant, Uttarakhand 248002

GEHU Dehradun campus provides an ideal setting for academic excellence, with modern facilities including computer centers, classrooms, accommodation, medical services, and a radio station. It has received recognition at national and international events.

About Graphic Era Hill University:

Established in 1996, Graphic Era Hill University has emerged as a leading institution for higher education in North India. Renowned for its academic excellence, research focus, and industry collaborations, GEHU provides a holistic learning experience for students across various disciplines. The university's commitment to sustainability and social responsibility aligns with the values of BSides Dehradun.

Getting There:

Graphic Era Hill University is conveniently located in Clement Town, Dehradun, and is accessible by various modes of transportation.

By Air

The nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport, approximately 30 km away. Taxis and pre-paid cabs are readily available from the airport.

By Train

Dehradun Railway Station is well-connected to major cities across India. The university is a short taxi or auto-rickshaw ride away from the station.

By Road

Dehradun is well-connected by road to major cities in North India. Regular bus services and private taxis are available.

Local Transportation

Auto-rickshaws, cycle-rickshaws, and shared taxis are popular modes of transportation within the city.

Parking:

Graphic Era Hill University offers ample parking space for attendees.

Accommodations:

While Graphic Era Hill University does not offer on-site accommodations, there are several hotels and guesthouses located nearby in Clement Town and other areas of Dehradun. We recommend booking your stay in advance, especially during peak season.
